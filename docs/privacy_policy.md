# Privacy Policy - Forest Merge 2048

Last updated: 2026-03-09

## 1) Overview
Forest Merge 2048 ("the App") respects user privacy. This policy explains what data is processed and why.

## 2) Data Processed
- Anonymous authentication ID (Firebase Auth anonymous UID)
- Gameplay/leaderboard data you submit (score, max tile, timestamp)
- App analytics events (screen views, gameplay events) via Firebase Analytics
- Ad-related data handled by Google AdMob (for ad delivery and measurement)
- Local on-device save data (points, inventory, upgrades, best score) in local storage

## 3) What We Do Not Collect Directly
- No direct collection of real name, phone number, or email inside the app
- No direct collection of payment card data by the app

## 4) Purpose of Processing
- Game operation (save/load, ranking)
- Service quality improvement (analytics)
- Ad serving and reward ad flow (AdMob)

## 5) Third-Party Services
- Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Analytics)
- Google AdMob

These services may process data according to their own policies.

## 6) Data Storage
- Local game progress is stored on your device.
- Ranking data is stored in Firebase Firestore.

## 7) User Choices
- You can clear app data/uninstall to remove local data.
- Network-off mode may limit ranking/online sync.

## 8) Children
The app is not intentionally directed to children under the age required by applicable law without parental consent.
